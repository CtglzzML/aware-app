# AWARE.

A mobile-first expense tracker for trips, nights out, events, activities, and casual plans.

## Why I Built This

I made AWARE. because I wanted a faster way to keep control of my spending when I go somewhere.

The idea is simple: when you are on a trip, at a party, in a cabin, at a concert, or just out for the weekend, money disappears quickly and it is very easy to lose track of what the whole plan actually costs.

I wanted something that felt:

- fast to open
- easy to use on mobile
- simple enough to log things in seconds
- good enough to capture expenses by voice when typing is annoying

The goal was never to build a heavy finance app. The goal was to build something light, sharp, and practical enough to answer one question:

How much did that actually cost?

## Easter Eggs

AWARE. has a few hidden interactions built into the app on purpose.

They are there to make the product feel a bit more alive, a bit less obvious, and a bit more fun for curious people.

Current easter eggs:

- On the landing page, tapping `aware.` cycles the accent color across the app.
- On the landing page, tapping the main quote changes it to another one with a typing-style reveal.
- On internal pages, tapping `aware.` takes you back to the landing page.
- In the dashboard, tapping the `Analysis` insight opens the hidden quote suggestion flow.

Some parts of AWARE. are meant to be discovered, not announced.
